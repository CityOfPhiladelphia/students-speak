Students Speak Up! Contest

Students Speak Up! will be an opportunity for Philadelphia’s public school students to share their voice around the importance of school funding. Students will submit an essay (250 – 500 words) or video to the Students Speak Up! Website responding to the following prompt –

Fully fund my education because…

Prizes

Select winners in each grade category (Elementary, Middle, and High School) will be rewarded with prizes such as box tickets to the Eagles opening game or an Apple Watch.
